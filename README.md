##  Project Overview
This project is developed as part of a Digital Literacy course to demonstrate practical understanding of essential digital skills required in today’s technology-driven world. It focuses on building competencies such as digital content creation, professional online presence, coding practice, digital communication, and cyber safety awareness.

The project is based on a student-centric scenario where a college learner actively uses digital platforms to enhance skills, collaborate, and maintain a responsible digital identity.

---

### Module Summaries

### **Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness**
Created a digital infographic to present key digital literacy concepts in a clear and visually engaging format using design tools.

### **Task 2: Professional Digital Identity**
Developed and explored professional profiles using platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to showcase skills, projects, and networking presence.

### **Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
Practiced programming problems on HackerRank to improve logical thinking, coding skills, and problem-solving ability.

### **Task 4: Digital Communication**
Studied digital communication practices including email etiquette and analyzed a real-life scenario demonstrating the impact of poor communication.

### **Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness**
Researched a cybercrime scenario (UPI/online fraud), created a case study, and developed a prevention checklist along with personal reflections on online safety.
