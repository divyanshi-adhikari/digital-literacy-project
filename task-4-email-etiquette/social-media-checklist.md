# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
- Think carefully before posting anything online
- Maintain a respectful and positive tone in all interactions
- Protect your personal information and privacy
- Verify facts before sharing any information
- Use social media for learning, networking, and personal growth

##  Don’ts
- Do not share fake or misleading information
- Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language
- Do not overshare personal or sensitive details
- Avoid engaging in unnecessary online arguments
- Do not post content that can harm your reputation
